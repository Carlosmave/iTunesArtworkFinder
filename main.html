iTunes Artwork Finder

Artwork Link

Instructions: Search in Google for the iTunes media content you want and open the webpage. Right click on the image thumbnail at the left and click 'View Image' (it may vary depending on your web browser). Once the image is open, copy the link and paste it where indicated here. Select the type of content you want and then click 'Get Artwork'. The link with the artwork at the highest resolution available will appear below, just copy and paste it into your browser and there you go.
